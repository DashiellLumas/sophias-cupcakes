Welcome to Sophia's Cupcakes

Baking sweet memories.

For as long as I can remember, I have loved baking in the kitchen. Early on in life I discovered the joys of making cupcakes, and I loved to experiment with new flavors, creations and designs. In 2015 I decided to follow my passion and turn this little hobby of mine into a reality. My recipe box is constantly expanding with delicious recipes that I have discovered or created. Each flavor has a unique story that I look forward to sharing with you!
